Circulation Policy

Borrowing Privileges

Patron Type No. of Items Duration Renewal
Academic and Administrative Officials 10 Books 1 Month Renewable 3x
Pre-School, Grade School, High School, College Students 2-5 Books 1 Week Renewable 3x
Support Staff 2 Books 1 Week Renewable 3x

Borrowing

  • Academic staff must be in active status and students must be currently enrolled for the current academic term.
  • Get the book/s and proceed to the Circulation Counter together with the Identification Card (ID).
  • Lending privilege is non-transferable. A library user is prohibited from lending his/her ID to another user.
  • Magazines, newspapers, reference materials, and all other resources are for room use only.

Returning

  • Borrowed books must be returned on or before the due date, otherwise a corresponding overdue fine will be implemented.

Renewal of Borrowed Book/s

  • Borrowers are allowed to renew library materials for three consecutive times if the material is not on the reservation list of another borrower.

Overdue Fines

  • A fine of ₱2.00 for students and ₱5.00 for faculty per book, per day will be charged. This does not include Sundays, holidays, suspension of classes, and lockdown periods.
